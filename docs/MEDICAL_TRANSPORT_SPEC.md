## Pricing Notes
Use the standard MinnDrive pricing formula from MEMORY.md:
- (miles+2) × rate + (orig_mins+2) × rate = base, then × 1.20
- Standard: $1.80/mi + $0.60/min, min $42
- Most metro hospitals are within 30 minutes of MSP → ~$42–$75 range
- Display as "From $XX" with "gratuity included"

## Differentiation vs Competitors
- **vs. Medical transport vans**: MinnDrive is private car (Chrysler Pacifica), not shared van. No multi-stop routes. Door-to-door.
- **vs. Uber/Lyft**: Flat rate, no surge, 6-hour advance booking = reliability. Driver knows hospital campuses. Accommodates equipment.
- **vs. Ambulance/NEMT**: MinnDrive is non-emergency only. For patients who can sit in a vehicle. Not insurance-billed (cash/card pay).

## Content Tone
- Empathetic but not saccharine. These are stressed patients/families — be direct and reassuring.
- Emphasize reliability, on-time arrival, and "one less thing to worry about."
- No medical jargon. Write for the patient's spouse or adult child who's booking the ride.
