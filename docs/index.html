The Hazeltine Standard

How pro detailers work — without the attitude

We’re not guessing with dollar-store soap and one filthy bucket. These three non-negotiables protect your paint and your time.

pH-neutral chemistry

Wash and decon products matched to clear coat and trim — not whatever strips fastest. Alkaline abuse and mystery “foaming” soaps don’t live here.

Two-bucket wash

Rinse bucket + soap bucket with grit guards. Dirty water never gets a second pass on your panels — the single biggest cheat code against wash marring.

Microfiber-only drying

No squeegees on paint, no gas-station towels. Plush microfiber and controlled technique so you’re not trading dirt for shine.

The Craft

What “Detailed” Actually Means

The boring stuff — done right — is what separates a weekend wash from a Hazeltine finish.

  • ✓

    Decon Before Dressing

    Iron, tar, and embedded grit come out before anything “shiny” goes on — so you’re not sealing scratches under wax.

  • ✓

    Two-Bucket Discipline

    Clean water stays clean. Dirty mitts don’t boomerang back onto your panels.

  • ✓

    Trim & Glass Finished Last

    So product doesn’t migrate where it shouldn’t — especially around rubber, seals, and sensors.

  • ✓

    Interior Airflow & Drying

    Fabric and carpet get proper extraction and dry-down — not “wet dog” humidity locked in the cabin.

  • ✓

    Final Walk in Mixed Light

    We QC in daylight-biased and shadow-biased angles — where cheap jobs fall apart first.

Mobile FAQ

Quick answers before we roll up — same points as our structured data for search.

Do you need my water or power?

We can work with your exterior water spigot and standard outlets when available; if your site cannot support that, we will note it when you request a quote and confirm logistics before we arrive.

Weather policy (about 40°F minimum)

Exterior work is scheduled when ambient conditions allow safe results — generally about 40°F minimum for many exterior steps. We may reschedule if ice, heavy rain, or extreme cold would compromise the finish or your vehicle.

How much space do you need?

A flat area where we can safely open all doors and access the vehicle — typically a driveway, wide parking stall, or similar. Tight garages may work for interior-only work; tell us your layout in the quote request.

FAQ

Common questions

Do you offer mobile auto detailing in the Twin Cities?

Yes. Hazeltine is 100% mobile — we detail at your location across Minneapolis, St. Paul, and 120+ surrounding communities. Book online or call (763) 352-7411 for availability.

What is the difference between a car wash and paint correction?

A tunnel wash removes loose dirt; paint correction uses machine polishing to reduce swirls, oxidation, and defects before sealant or ceramic coating is applied. It is the right choice when you want depth and clarity in direct sunlight.

How much does ceramic coating cost in Minnesota?

Ceramic coating at Hazeltine starts around $899 and includes proper paint preparation. Final price depends on vehicle size, paint condition, and whether multi-stage correction is required.

Do you detail interiors with pet hair or salt stains?

Yes. Interior packages include extraction, pet hair remediation, and Minnesota road-salt tracking cleanup. Heavy contamination is quoted after photos or an on-site inspection.
