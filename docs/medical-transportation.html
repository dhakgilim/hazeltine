What We Offer

Medical Rides, Done Right

Everything you need to know about your ride to the hospital.

♿ Patient-Friendly Vehicle

Our Chrysler Pacifica has wide doors, low step-in height, and room for walkers, folding wheelchairs, oxygen tanks, and medical equipment. Tell us what you need when you book.

💰 Flat Rate Pricing

Your fare is confirmed before you book. No surge pricing, no hidden fees, no meter running. Gratuity is already included — the price you see is the price you pay.

⏳ Wait-Time Service

For same-day round trips, your driver can wait on-site during your appointment. $75/hour, billed in 15-minute increments. Walk out and your car is right there.

🔁 Recurring Schedules

Dialysis, chemotherapy, physical therapy, ongoing check-ups — book a standing schedule and get the same driver when possible. Same flat rate, every time.

🏥 Campus Drop-off

Your driver knows the hospital campus — the right entrance, the patient drop-off zone, and where to park if they're waiting. No circling the lot, no confusion.

📞 Easy Changes

Appointment moved? Discharge delayed? Call us and we'll adjust your pickup. Flexibility is built in — we work around your medical schedule, not the other way around.
